Why did I become a celebrant?

I’ve always loved everything about weddings! I mean who wouldn’t love a big party where all of your favourite people are there to celebrate your decision to spend the rest of your life with your best friend? When I was planning my own wedding in 2018, I developed a real passion for the wedding industry and knew I wanted to be part of it. What better way than to be able to officiate weddings for loved up couples?! It’s a great honour and privilege to be able to help share a couple’s story with their loved ones. Your wedding day really is one of the best days of your life and it’s so special that I get to play a part in that. I really am a sucker for a good love story! I find great pleasure in being able to help a couple tell their story by writing a personalised and unique ceremony. Getting married really is a wild adventure. One to be enjoyed and treasured. I do love a good adventure… so let the adventure begin and get in touch. I’d love to hear your story!
