WEDDING CEREMONY

The Full Service

$800

Your big day is a big deal to me! Your wedding ceremony sets the tone for your day which is why it’s so important for me to create a unique ceremony that reflects who you are as a couple. This ceremony is tailored to you. I will custom write your ceremony & cater to your individual needs to ensure your ceremony aligns with the vision you have for your big day.

WHAT'S INCLUDED?

  • Preparation and lodgement of all legal documentation.
  • Creation of your unique ceremony that will be created in partnership with you.
  • Access to resources that allow for ceremonial enhancement. This includes access to elements such as; readings, rituals and inspiration for your personal vows.
  • Professional delivery of your ceremony with the use of my PA system, which will also be used to play your ceremonial music.
  • I will arrive at least 45 minutes prior to the commencement of your ceremony & touch base with all the key players of your day to ensure everyone is organised and comfortable with their role for the day.
  • Professional advice on the staging and choreography of your wedding ceremony, including an onsite rehearsal.
  • 2-3 face-to-face meetings & unlimited communication via phone or email to assist you with any questions, concerns or wedding planning questions you may have.
  • All travel costs associated with attending meetings, the rehearsal & your ceremony (up to 80km).

I also offer the following ceremonies…

RENEWAL OF VOWS

Already married and want to reaffirm your love for one another? Renewing your vows is a really special way to celebrate an anniversary or just a lovely excuse to remind your loved one of how much they mean to you.

LEGALS ONLY CEREMONY

Having a destination wedding overseas where your marriage isn’t recognised and want to make it legal here in Aus? Or you don’t want the fuss of a full-blown ceremony? A legals only ceremony may be the option for you! This ceremony includes the preparation and lodgement of all legal paperwork & a short ceremony that is no longer than 5 minutes where you will need to hear me say my legal spiel (called the Monitum), read legal vows to one another and have x2 witnesses over the age of 18.

NAMING CEREMONY

A non-religious, no legalities, symbolic celebration for your little one. This ceremony is a lovely way to introduce your child to the world. Naming ceremonies are a special way for parents to share their hopes and dreams for their child and to also recognise the commitment and promises they wish to make. There are many special and unique elements you can include in a naming ceremony & my resources will help you to customise your day to suit your needs.

COMMITMENT CEREMONY

A ceremony where you and your loved one can make promises to one another without it being legally binding. A really special way to affirm your love for one another.

Please get in contact with me with details of your day to discuss inclusions and pricing for these particular ceremonies.
